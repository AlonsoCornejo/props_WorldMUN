What is the O.P.E.N.E.D?

It is an extension database that takes information from UN databases, particularly the TradeMap which contains free information to worldwide imports and exports, and by using techniques of data mining and modelling provides clean information on commercial opportunities, patterns and other attributes that might be used by any user or organism to achieve multiple utopic goals. Moreover, the OPENED will use blockchain technology to ensure information provided is not biased or changed by any cybercriminal. At the end of the webpage you can try a basic representation of the project.

Benefits of OPENED:

  • Private Companies: Find new business approaches and ethically increase profit. Furthermore, this benefit will incentivize job formalization so companies can legally export, thus reducing illegal practices such as child labor, polluting production tecnhiques and many other ones.
  • Governments: Evaluate commercial treaties to increase GDP value and contribute to reaching economic independence.
  • United Nations: Get facts to elaborate its theory of colonialism.
